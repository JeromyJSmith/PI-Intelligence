Methodology

The 5-Stage Diligence Pipeline

A repeatable, layered process that moves from intake normalization through to a documented decision memo — verified at each stage.

Intake & Normalize
Collect legal name, aliases, DOB, locations, firms, and known associates. Build canonical case record.
Regulator Pass
IAPD, SEC enforcement, FINRA disclosure framework. High-signal structured records checked first.
Federal Court Pass
PACER Case Locator — civil, bankruptcy, appellate. Search individual and all tied entities.
Alias & Associate Expansion
Name variants, prior addresses, domains, usernames. People-search as lead generator — verify in primary sources.
Evidence & Decision Memo
Classify by severity and confidence. Separate allegations from findings. Produce a final decision memo.
Priority Resources

Key Databases & Platforms

The highest-signal resources, organized by category, cost, and role in the pipeline.

Category Resource Cost Pipeline Role
Adviser checks IAPD / adviserinfo.sec.gov Free Confirm adviser registration, disclosures, and firm identity
SEC enforcement SEC Action Lookup & Litigation Releases Free Identify enforcement actions and related litigation history
Broker history FINRA BrokerCheck Free Reportable events: complaints, judgments, liens, bankruptcies
Federal litigation PACER Case Locator Freemium Civil, bankruptcy, and appellate records nationwide
People search BeenVerified, TruthFinder, Intelius, Instant Checkmate Paid Lead generation for aliases, addresses, associates — not final proof
Company records OCCRP Aleph · OpenCorporates Free Cross-border entity links, beneficial ownership, sanctions
OSINT investigation Miessler investigation skills · LangGraph OSINT agents Open Source Reusable structures for person lookup, company intel, evidence handling
Operating principle: No single database is sufficient. Cross-check every material finding across at least two independent source types — for example, IAPD plus SEC enforcement, or a people-search hit plus PACER and public web corroboration.
